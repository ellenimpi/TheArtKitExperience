How It Works

Step 1: Visit 44 Gaukel, Kitchener, ON and take a picture of our empty canvas

Step 2: Use our motion sensor feature to create your own art only using your creativity starting from your fingertips.

Step 3: Save your new piece of art with the Artkit app, and upload to our database

Step 4: You're done! Your artpiece will now be shown to the world!
